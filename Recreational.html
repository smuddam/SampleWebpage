Recreational Bike:

Recreation is an activity of leisure, leisure being discretionary time spent on riding a bike. The "need to do something for recreation" is an essential element of human biology and psychology. Recreational activities are often done for enjoyment, amusement, or pleasure and are considered to be "fun".
